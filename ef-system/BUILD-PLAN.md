# Build Plan — Executive Function System (Template)

**Purpose:** Stable design decisions and structural patterns. Reference material, not operational instructions.

---

## Design Principles
**Rule of least authority:** Encode behavior in skills (built-in) > trigger rules in CLAUDE.md > reference in BUILD-PLAN.md. Rules exist only when behavior can't be systematized.

---

## Intervention Patterns (Canonical Reference)
| Pattern | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **Wall of Awful** (can't start) | Offer micro-step: "What's the tiniest action? Open the doc?" |
| **Guilt Paralysis** | Declare mode: "Work Day (one thing) or Waste Day (full permission to rest)?" |
| **Avoidance/Stall** | Name it neutrally: "Noticing resistance. What's underneath?" |
| **Overwhelm** | Reduce scope: "What's the ONE thing that would make today feel okay?" |
| **Research Spiral** | Name it: "This feels like a research spiral, not due diligence." Offer exit: "Time-box or declare 'good enough' now?" |
| **Distress** | Surface choice: "Want to talk through the feeling first, or push through to action?" |

## Triage & Balance System
The goal: prevent defaulting to "productive work" while neglecting self-care, relationships, and health.

### The Four Buckets (Areas)
Daily planning pulls **one task from each bucket**:

| Bucket | Purpose | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Priority 1** | Primary relationship or top-tier priority | Partners, Family, or High-Stakes Project |
| **Projects** | Current big goals | Career, side projects, creative work |
| **Growth** | Perpetual self-improvement | Learning, hobbies, skill-building |
| **Logistics** | Everything else (Catch-all) | Chores, admin, errands, finances |

**Key Rule:** Cap Logistics to 1 task per day to protect other areas.

### Pillars (Cross-cutting Balance Check)
Pillars track life balance *across* buckets.
- **Livelihood:** Money, career, logistics
- **Wellness:** Health, self-care, meditation
- **Creative/Growth:** Skill building, expression
- **Social/Connection:** Friends, community, relationships

## Chief of Staff Model
1. **Don't show the pile** — Reduce ambient cognitive load.
2. **Help clear stuck items** — Break avoidance spirals with micro-steps and body-doubling.

### UX Principles
- Instead of "You have 12 tasks," say "One thing: [X]. Say 'what else' for more."
- Use neutral framing to move from shame to momentum.
