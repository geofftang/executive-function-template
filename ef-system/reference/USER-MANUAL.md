# User Manual: Your AI Chief of Staff

Welcome to your personalized Executive Function (EF) system. This guide explains how the "gears" of this system turn to help you stay organized and focused.

## 1. The Shared Brain (Cross-Session Memory)
This system remembers your sessions so you don't have to repeat yourself.
- **How it works:** A memory database (claude-mem) stores key facts, decisions, and context across sessions.
- **The Magic:** When you start a new session, the AI automatically "catches up" on what you did earlier.
- **Key Command:** `@mem search [topic]` to find anything you've ever discussed.

## 2. The Knowledge Hub (PARA System)
Your files are organized into four simple "Buckets":
- **Projects:** Active work with a deadline (e.g., "Plan 2026 Trip").
- **Areas:** Long-term responsibilities (e.g., "Health", "Finances").
- **Resources:** Things you want to learn or reference (e.g., "Articles").
- **Archive:** Finished or abandoned work.

## 3. The Source of Truth (`STATE.md`)
This is the single most important file in your system.
- It tracks your **Daily Intention** (what you want to do today).
- It logs your **Weekly Goals**.
- **Tip:** If the AI is confused, tell it: "Read my STATE.md."

## 4. The Skill Suite (Your Commands)
Type `@help` anytime to see all available commands. Here are the essentials:

**Start here (daily use):**
- `@morning-planning` — Set your intentions for the day
- `@checkpoint` — Save progress and check alignment with goals
- `@task create` / `@task update` — Manage your tasks

**When you need them:**
- `@plan` — Audit a task before starting (catches gaps, estimates friction)
- `@new-project` — Set up a new project with proper structure
- `@digest [url or text]` — Process an article, email, or content
- `@support` — Get help when stuck, overwhelmed, or spiraling

**You don't need to memorize these.** The AI will suggest the right command when it recognizes what you're trying to do. If it doesn't, just describe what you need — it knows the full list.

## 5. Why this works
Traditional to-do lists are static—they don't understand your energy levels or your habits. This system is **Self-Healing**: it learns your "Breaking Points" and adjusts its personality (Sergeant vs. Coach) to keep you moving.
