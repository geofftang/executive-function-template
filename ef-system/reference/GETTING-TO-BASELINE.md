# Protocol: Getting to Baseline (The Onboarding)

This document is the "Baton" for setting up or resetting an Executive Function system to the unified standard.

## 1. The Reset (The Clean Room)
Before starting, provide your AI with this command:
> "I am upgrading to the unified Executive Function baseline. Please create a folder called `backups/v_old/`. Move my current `CLAUDE.md` and `skills/` folder into it. Do not touch my `projects/`, `areas/`, or `STATE.md` files."

## 2. The Standard Injection
Copy the template files into your system root. The template contains:
- `CLAUDE.md` (The Universal Standard)
- `ef-system/skills/` (The Skill Library)
- `ef-system/reference/` (Supporting docs)
- `ef-system/STATE.md` (Daily tracking scaffold)
- `projects/` and `resources/` (PARA folder scaffolds)

## 3. The Calibration (The Interview)
Open `ef-system/reference/setup/SETUP-GUIDE.md` and walk through Phase 1 (Discovery) with your AI. The interview covers:
- **Life Areas & Pillars:** How you organize your world.
- **Aspirations (WOOP):** Your North Star and internal obstacle defense plan.
- **Energy Mapping:** Identifying your biological Peak and Valley windows.
- **Struggles & Failure Mechanics:** Mapping your specific breaking points (Research Abyss, False Start, Guilt Spiral, etc.).
- **Working Preferences:** Modality, automation tolerance, granularity.
- **Support Style:** Choosing the archetype (Warm Coach, Direct Commander, Sardonic Companion, or Neutral Professional) that keeps you moving.

After the interview, Phase 2 generates your personalized config files.

## 4. Why this matters
This session is designed to best understand how your AI can help you live your life to the fullest. The system calibrates to your specific energy patterns, failure mechanisms, and communication style — not a one-size-fits-all template.
